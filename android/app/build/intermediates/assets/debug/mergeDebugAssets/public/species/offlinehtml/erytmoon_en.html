Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees .
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; stipules in pairs 0.3 cm long, lanceolate , caducous and leaving scar ; petiole 0.2-0.4 cm long, planoconvex in cross section; lamina 4-8 x 1.5-3 cm, elliptic , apex caudate - acuminate , base acute ; margin entire , glabrous , chartaceous ; midrib canaliculate above; secondary obscure and tertiary_nerves not visible.
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers axillary , solitary .
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , narrow ellipsoid ; 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Rare understorey trees in medium elevation evergreen forests ca. 900 m.

Distribution :

Western_Ghats and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- South Sahyadri and Wayanad in Central Sahyadri.

Literatures :

Bull. Inst. Bot. Buitenzorg 22: 54. 1905; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 127. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 66. 2004.
