Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 25 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; petioles ca. 1.2 cm long; lamina 12.5-18 x 3-5 cm, lanceolate , apex gradually acuminate with blunt tip, base acute , margin entire ; midrib raised above; secondary_nerves 10-12 pairs; tertiary_nerves closely_horizontally_percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary panicles ; flowers yellowish white.
Fruit and Seed :Nut , ca. 1.2 cm across, globose , apiculate , enclosed at the base of thickened and accrescent_calyx_lobes ; seed 1.

Ecology :

Canopy trees in dry evergreen forests between 300 and 600 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- confine to leeward side of Agasthyamalai Hills.

Kew Bull. 146. 1951; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 84. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 45. 2004.
