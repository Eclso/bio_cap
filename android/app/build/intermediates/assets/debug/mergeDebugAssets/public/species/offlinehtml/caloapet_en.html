Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 30 m tall.
Leaves :Simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 0.3-0.5 cm long, planoconvex in cross section, glabrous ; lamina 4-8 x 2-4.5 cm, usually obovate to oblanceolate , apex rounded to retuse , base rounded or subtruncate , coriaceous , glossy above; secondary_nerves numerous , close, parallel , terminating at the thick margin.
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers white in axillary panicle .
Fruit and Seed :Drupe yellowish brown, ovoid to ellipsoid , to 1.5 cm long, 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Usually along streams in low elevation wet evergreen forests up to 900 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :

Ges. Naturf. Freunde Berlin Mag. 5: 79. 1811; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 76. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 39. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 1: 202. 1996.
