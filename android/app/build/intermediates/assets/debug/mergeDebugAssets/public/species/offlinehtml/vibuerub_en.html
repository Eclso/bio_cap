Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees , up to 8 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; stipule lanceolate , caducous ; petiole reddish, to 3 cm long, canaliculate ; lamina to 12 x 8 cm, broadly elliptic to obovate , apex acute-acuminate , base cuneate to rounded , margin distally serrate to dentate , all nerves impressed above; midrib canaliculate ; secondary_nerves 5-7 pairs, subopposite ; tertiary_nerves percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence in drooping panicles ; flowers white.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , red, oblong , 1.5 x 0.8 cm; seed one, oblong , 1 cm compressed with a median, T-shaped ventral groove .

Ecology :

Along the margin in the high altitude evergreen forests between 1800 and 2200 m.

Distribution :

India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- Anaimalai, Palani and Nilgiris.
