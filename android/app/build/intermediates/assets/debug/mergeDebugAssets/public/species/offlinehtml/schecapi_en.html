Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 15 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves compound , digitate , wooly when young, alternate , spiral , pulvinate with sheathing base, stipule adnate to the petiole ; petiolules up to 5 cm long; leaflets 6-8 (to 11), lamina 8-17.5 x 3.8-5.5 cm, elliptic-oblong , apex acute or acuminate , base rounded or truncate , coriaceous ; midrib raised above; secondary_nerves 13-16 pairs, slender; tertiary_nerves broadly reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flower umbels in panicles of racemes on stout branches .
Fruit and Seed :Drupe ; seeds compressed.

Ecology :

Canopy trees in high altitude (1400-2200 m) evergreen forests and along margin in medium elevation (800-1400 m) evergreen forests.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- common in Nilgiris, Anaimalai and Palani Hills and occasional in other parts South and Central Sahyadris.
