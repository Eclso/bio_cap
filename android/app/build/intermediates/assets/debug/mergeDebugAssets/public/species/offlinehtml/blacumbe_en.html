Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate or subopposite or sometimes spiral ; stipules caducous ; petiole 0.5-4.5 cm long, canaliculate , glabrous ; lamina 5-15 x 2.5-4.5 cm, elliptic to obovate to oblanceolate , apex acuminate or caudate - acuminate , base gradually acute to cuneate , margin entire , subcoriaceous , glabrous ; midrib raised above; secondary_nerves 6-8 pairs; tertiary_nerves admedially_ramified .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers unisexual ; male flowers in slender pedunculate umbels ; female flowers terminal fascicles .
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , globose , deeply 3-lobed ; fruiting_calyx erect; seeds 3.

Ecology :

Understorey trees in evergreen forests up to 1100 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- South Sahyadri and Central Sahyadri (up to Coorg Region).

Literatures :

Etud. Gen. Euphorb. 387. 18. 1858; Gamble, Fl. Madras 2: 1338. 1993 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 411. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 2: 121. 1996.
