Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 0.5-0.7 cm long, canaliculate above, glabrous ; lamina 4-8.5 x 0.6-1.4 cm, linear-lanceolate , apex gradully acute to acuminate , base acute to attenuate , margin entire , pellucid gland dotted, glabrous beneath, coriaceous ; midrib slightly canaliculate ; secondary_nerves many, oblique ; intramarginal nerve present; tertiary_nerves admedially ramified .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary and terminal cymes ; flowers small, white; calyx tube funnel shaped, ca. 0.5 cm long; pedicel ca. 0.2 cm long, slender.
Fruit and Seed :Berry , crowned with calyx segments; seed one.

Ecology :

Trees along streams up to 1400 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Agasthyamalai.

Literatures :

Prodr. 3: 260. 1828; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 479.1997 (re.ed).
