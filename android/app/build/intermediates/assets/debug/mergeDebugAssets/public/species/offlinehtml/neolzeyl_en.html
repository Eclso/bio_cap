Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 20 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral , clustered at twig ends; petiole 1.2-2 cm long, planoconvex in cross section, glabrous ; lamina 7-11 x 3-3.5 cm, elliptic to narrow elliptic , apex narrowly acute to acuminate , base attenuate , glaucous beneath, subcoriaceous ; midrib raised above; trinerved just above the base with ca. 4 pairs of secondary_nerves ; tertiary_nerves distantly reticulo-percurrent ; higher order nerves closely reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence umbels , axillary or lateral clusters.
Fruit and Seed :Berry , ellipsoid-oblong ; fruiting_perianth scarcely accrescent and crenate ; seed 1.

Ecology :

Common understorey to subcanopy trees in evergreen forests, up to 1800 m.

Distribution :

Indomalaysia; in the Western_Ghats- South, Central and south Maharashtra Sahyadris.

Literatures :

Gamble, Fl.Madras 1240: 1925; Gamble, Fl. Madras 2: 1239. 1993 (re. ed); Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 1: 71. 1996.
