Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees , up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite to subopposite , decussate ; petiole 0.5-1 cm long, canaliculate ; lamina 7-15 x 2.5-6.5 cm, elliptic or oblong-lanceolate , acuminate , base acute to attenuate , margin entire , margins reflexed , pale beneath, glabrous , coriaceous ; secondary_nerves ca. 8 pairs, not prominent; tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in sessile clusters, axillary .
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , ellipsoid , 1.5 cm long; 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Understorey trees in medium elevation evergreen forests, between 700 and 1500m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Central Sahyadri (Palakkad hills, South Malanad and Central Malanad).

Literatures :

Hooker, Fl. Brit. Ind. 1: 613. 1875; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 206. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 97. 2004; Cook, Fl. Bombay 1: 229. 1902.
