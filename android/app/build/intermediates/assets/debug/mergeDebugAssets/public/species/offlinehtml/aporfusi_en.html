Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees .
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; stipules caducous ; petiole 0.5-1 cm long, canaliculate ; lamina 5.5-8 x 3-5.5 cm, obovate or orbicular , apex obtuse or retuse , base cuneate ; midrib slightly raised above; secondary_nerves ca. 5 pairs; tertiary_nerves reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers unisexual , dioecious ; male flowers in axillary catkins , 2.5 cm long; female flowers in condensed cymes .
Fruit and Seed :Capsule fusiform , glabrous , beaked , 2 cm long, 2-4 seeded .

Ecology :

Understorey trees in evergreen forests.

Distribution :

India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- Central Sahyadris.
