Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees , up to 8 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral , clustered at twig ends; petiole 0.6-1.2 cm long, flat above, glabrous ; lamina 4-15 x 2.3-7.5 cm, obovate , apex obtuse or rounded , base cuneate , margin undulate , coriaceous , glabrous , midrib flat above; secondary_nerves 5-8 pairs; tertiary_nerves reticulo-percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flower heads in terminal panicles , purple.
Fruit and Seed :Achenes ribbed with yellow glands ; 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Along margins in medium elevation evergreen forests between 1000 and 1700 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Agasthyamalai and Varushanad Hills of South Sahyadri.

Literatures :

Fl. Brit. India 3: 240.1881; Gamble, Fl. Madras 2: 673. 1993 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 257. 2004.
