Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 30 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral , sometimes subopposite , clustered at twig ends; petioles 1-1.5 cm; lamina 9.5-15 x 3.5-6 cm, elliptic to ovate-lanceolate , apex acute to shortly acuminate with blunt tip, base acute to attenuate , margin entire , glabrous , smooth and shining above, coriaceous ; secondary_nerves ca.10 pairs, slender, gradually curved; tertiary_nerves strongly reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in short axillary panicles .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , ellipsoid , ca. 1.7 cm long; fruiting pedicel stout, red; fruiting_perianth caducous .

Ecology :

Canopy trees in evergreen forests up to 1200 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- between Central and south Maharashtra Sahyadris.

DC., Prodr. 15 (1): 64. 1864; Gamble, Fl. Madras 2: 1221. 1993 (re. ed); Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 1: 59. 1996.
