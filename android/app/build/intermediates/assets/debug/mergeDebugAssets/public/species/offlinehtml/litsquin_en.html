Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 6 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; petiole 1.5 cm long, planoconvex in cross section, glabrous ; lamina 7-10 x 2.5-4 cm, obovate to oblanceolate or elliptic , subcoriaceous , glabrous , apex obtuse to acute , base acute to cuneate , margin entire , glabrous , glaucous beneath; secondary_nerves 6-9 pairs; higher order nerves reticulate on both sides at least on dry.
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in axillary or lateral umbels .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , 0.6 cm across, glabrous ; seed 1.

Ecology :

Generally in dry evergreen forests between 700 and 2200 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- rare in South Sahyadri, Palakkad hills and Wayanad.
