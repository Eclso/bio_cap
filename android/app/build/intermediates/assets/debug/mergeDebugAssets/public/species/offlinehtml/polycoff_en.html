Tamil: Nakulai, Nedunarai. Malayalam: Nedunar, Villa.

Guatteria coffeoides Thw.

Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees , up to up to 20 m.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; petiole 0.6-1 cm long, stout; lamina 10-27 x 3.5-8.5 cm, lanceolate , sometimes narrow elliptic , apex acuminate , base acute , and sometimes slightly oblique on one side, margin undulate , shining above; secondary_nerves 9-16 pairs, ascending; tertiary_nerves filiform , percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers solitary or in clusters on branchlets and on tubercles of trunk , greenish yellow with red tinge.
Fruit and Seed :Cluster of 1-seeded berries .

Ecology :

Subcanopy trees in low and medium elevation evergreen forests up to 1200 m.

Distribution :

Western_Ghats (South and Central Sahyadris) and Sri Lanka.

Literatures :

Hooker, Fl. Brit. India 1: 62. 1872; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 16. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 19. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 1: 48. 1984; Keshava Murthy and Yoganarasimhan, Fl. Coorg (Kodagu) 33. 1990.
