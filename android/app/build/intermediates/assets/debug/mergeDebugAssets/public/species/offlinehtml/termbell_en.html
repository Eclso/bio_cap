Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Large deciduous trees , buttressed , up to 40 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral , clustered at the twig ends; petiole 3-10 cm long; flat above, with pair of sessile glands just above the middle, sometimes indistinct; lamina 8-20 x 4-14 cm, broad elliptic or broad obovate , apex rounded to abruptly short acuminate , base cuneate , margin entire , coriaceous , pubescent , when young on both sides, later glabrous ; midrib flat above; secondary_nerves 6-8 pairs, gradually curved; tertiary_nerves reticulo-percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary spikes ; flowers sessile , creamy white.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , globose or ovoid , slightly 5 ridged, 3 cm across; 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Emergent in the openings of evergreen to semi-evergreen forests up to 1400 m.

Distribution :

Indomalaysia; through out the Western_Ghats .
