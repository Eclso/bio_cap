Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 12 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; stipule caducous ; petiole 1.5-7.6 cm long, inflated at both ends, pubescent , terete , striate ; lamina 6-22 x 3-11.5 cm, ovate to ovate-lanceolate , apex acuminate , base acute to rounded , margin entire or serrate , chartaceous , glabrous above, pubescent with red resinous punctate beneath; trinerved_at_base ; secondary_nerves ca. 7 pairs; tertiary_nerves percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers unisexual , dioecious , in terminal spikes .
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , globose , densely red glandular ; seeds black, 1 per cell.

Ecology :

Common understorey trees in evergreen to semi-evergreen forests up to 1500 m.

Distribution :

Indomalaysia and Australia; in the Western_Ghats- through out.
