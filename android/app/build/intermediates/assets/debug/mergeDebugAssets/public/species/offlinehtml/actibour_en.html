Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 10 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral , subverticilate ; petiole 0.8-1.2 cm long, flat, densely fulvous tomentose ; lamina 11-24 x 2-6.3 cm, narrowly long elliptic-oblanceolate , apex narrowly acute to acuminate ; base narrowly cuneate , margin entire , chartaceous , densely tomentose on midrib and nerves beneath, glabrous above except midrib ; midrib raised above; secondary_nerves 7-14 pairs, very oblique ; tertiary_nerves closely_horizontally_percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers unisexual , dioecious , in umbels on short peduncle .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , black when ripe, seated on cup shaped fruiting_perianth ; seed 1.

Ecology :

Understorey trees in wet evergreen forests between 900 and 2400 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- South Sahyadri and Palakkad Hills.

Literatures :

Kew Bull. 129. 1925; Gamble, Fl. Madras 2: 1231. 1993 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 394. 2004.
