Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 15 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 1-2 cm long, canaliculate ; lamina 6-12 x 3-5.8 cm, elliptic to elliptic-obovate , apex acuminate , base attenuate , margin entire and slightly wavy , glabrous , chartaceous ; midrib slightly canaliculate ; secondary_nerves 4-8 pairs; hairy domatia present in the axils; tertiary_nerves reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence trichotomous cymes , terminal or axillary ; flowers bisexual , cream-white.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , ovoid , brownish-black when ripe; seed one.

Ecology :

Understorey trees in evergreen and semi-evergreen forests between 1200 and 2200 m.

Distribution :

India, Himalaya and Nepal; in the Western_Ghats- Anaimalai, Nilgiris and Bababudangiri hills.

Literatures :

DC, Prodr. 8: 294. 1844; Gamble, Fl. Madras 2: 796.1993 (re.ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 279. 2004.
