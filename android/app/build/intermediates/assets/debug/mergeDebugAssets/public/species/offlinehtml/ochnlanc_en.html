Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees up to 8 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; stipules caducous and leaving scar ; petioles ca. 0.3 cm long, planoconvex , glabrous ; lamina 4.5-8 x1.2-1.8 cm, narrow elliptic , elliptic-lanceolate , apex gradually acute , base acute , margin serrate , shining above, subcoriaceous , glabrous beneath; midrib raised above; secondary_nerves ca. 12 pairs; tertiary_nerves slender, reticulo-percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence solitary or in racemes of 2-3 flowers , axillary or lateral ; pedicels up to 2.5 cm long.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , 5-7 distinct drupes seated on the enlarged disk ; seeds 1 drupe , erect, oblong .
