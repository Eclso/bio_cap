Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees , up to 8 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole ca. 0.7 cm long; lamina 3-9.3 x 1.6-4.7 cm, elliptic to ovate , apex obtuse , base rounded to cuneate , coriaceous , pale beneath, olive-grey when dry; secondary_nerves 6-8 pairs, slender; tertiary_nerves admedially_ramified .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence cymes , 4-8 cm long.
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , brown, apiculate , sepals persistent in fruit ; 1-seeded , arillate .

Ecology :

Subcanopy trees , generally along the margin in high altitude evergreen forests, between 1600 and 2300 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- common in Nilgiris and also recorded in Palani Hills and Agasthyamalai Hills.
