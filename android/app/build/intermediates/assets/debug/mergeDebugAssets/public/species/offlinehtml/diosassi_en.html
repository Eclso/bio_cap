Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 30 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; petiole to 1 cm long, flat above, glabrous ; lamina 7-15.2 x 2.5-6 cm, narrow oblong to oblong-elliptic , apex acuminate with blunt tip, base rounded , margin entire coriaceous to subcoriaceous ; midrib slightly impressed above; secondary_nerves 4-7 pairs, ascending, lower most 2 pairs of nerves oblique and close; tertiary_nerves reticulate or weakly percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers dioecious ; male flowers white, in axillary clusters or in short cymes , tubular; female flowers solitary , sessile .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , globose ; fruiting_calyx inner rim raised; lobes reflexed ; seeds 6.

Ecology :

Canopy trees in low elevation wet evergreen forest up to 900 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.
