Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Large shrubs or small trees , up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 0.3-0.7 cm, flat above, glabrous ; lamina 8-15 x 2.5-6.2 cm, elliptic to narrow elliptic , apex acuminate , base acute to cuneate , margin serrate ( serrations starts 1-2.5 cm above the base), chartaceous , glabrous ; midrib slightly raised above; secondary_nerves ca. 8 pairs; tertiary_nerves very slender, broadly reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in axillary or extra-axillary dichasial cymes , pale greenish-yellow.
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , 1-4 celled , smooth; seeds arillate .

Ecology :

Understorey tree in wet evergreen forests between 500 and 900 m.

Distribution :

Peninsular India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- rarely found in Agasthyamalai, Anaimalai, Palakkad hills.
