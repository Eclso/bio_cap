Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 12 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves compound , imparipinnate , to 25 cm, alternate , spiral ; rachis to 5 cm; leaflets up to 10 pairs with terminal one, opposite ; petiolule of the lateral leaflets to 4 mm, terminal one to 2 cm; lamina 11-15 x 4.5-5 cm, oblong-lanceolate , chartaceous , glaucous , glabrous , apex acuminate , base oblique , cuneate , margin serrate ; secondary_nerves ca. 15 pairs; tertiary_nerves reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary panicles , to 20 cm, peduncle up to 5 cm.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , globose to lobed , up to 1.5 cm across; 1 seeded per pyrene .

Ecology :

Occasional in medium and high elevation evergreen forests, between 700 and 1600 m.

Distribution :

India to West China; in the Western_Ghats- South Sahyadris and Nilgiris in Tamil Nadu Ghats.

Literatures :

Blumea 7: 466. 1953; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 169. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 86. 2004.
