Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 20 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; petiole ca. 1 cm long, planoconvex in cross section, glabrous ; lamina 7.5-13 x 2.5-4 cm, elliptic-oblong to lanceolate , apex acute to obtuse , base acute to rounded , margin entire , glabrous , glaucous beneath; midrib canaliculate ; secondary_nerves 6-8 pairs; tertiary_nerves broadly reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in axillary or terminal panicle , pubescent .
Fruit and Seed :Drupaceous , ovoid , lenticellate , apparently ribbed when dry, 1.5 cm across; seed 1.

Ecology :

Canopy trees in high elevation evergreen forests generally between 1400 and 2200 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Nilgiris, Anaimalai and Shimoga (rare).

Literatures :
