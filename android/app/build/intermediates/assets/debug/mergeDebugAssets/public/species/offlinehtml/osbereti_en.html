Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Shrubs ca. 4 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 1 cm long, planoconvex in cross section, ferruginous hairy ; lamina 5-8 x 2.5-4.5 cm, ovate , apex acute , base obtuse to subcordate , margin entire , coriaceous , dark green above, paler beneath, densely tawny - bristly on both upper and lower surfaces; midrib canaliculate above; 5-7 nerved at base.
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence terminal , 4-6 flowered; flowers purple; hypanthium urceolate , 1.5 x 1.4 cm, with tufts of wooly bristles .
Fruit and Seed :Capsule urceolate ; seeds numerous .

Ecology :

Small trees in the open grass lands and edges of the stunted evergreen forests at high altitude between 1800 and 2400 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the southern Western_Ghats- Anaimalai and Palani hills in South Sahyadris.
