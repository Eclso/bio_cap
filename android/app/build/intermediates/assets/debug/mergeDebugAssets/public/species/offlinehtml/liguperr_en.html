Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , distichous , sometimes subopposite ; petiole up to 1 cm long, canaliculate ; lamina 1.5-5 (7.5) x 1.2-3.2 (-5) cm, elliptic to elliptic-obovate , apex acute or short acuminate with mucronate tip, base rounded to attenuate , margin entire , chartaceous , glabrous beneath; midrib canaliculate above; secondary_nerves 3-6 pairs, slender; tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence terminal thyrsoid panicles , stout, short, dense, glabrous ; flowers white, fragrant.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , obovoid , ca. 2 cm long, dark purple; seeds 1-3.

Ecology :

Along margin of semi-evergreen forests up to 2300 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to Western_Ghats- throughout.
