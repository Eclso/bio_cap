Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees , up to 8 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; petiole to 0.25 cm long, pubescent ; lamina 4-10 x 1.5- 2.5 cm long, narrow oblong-elliptic to lanceolate , apex acuminate , base rounded-acute , entire , chartaceous ; glabrous ; midrib hairy above; secondary_nerves 5-7 pairs, slender; tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers solitary , axillary , pinkish; pedicels 1.5-2.5 cm long, glabrous .
Fruit and Seed :Clustered 1-2 seeded berries , 1.2 cm long, oblong to globose with elongated stalk .

Ecology :

Understorey tree in wet evergreen forests at 700-1500 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to Western_Ghats- eastern slopes of Agasthyamalai Hills, Varushanad Hills Nilgiris, Coorg Region and South Malanad.

Literatures :

Hooker and Thomson, Fl. Ind. 149. 1855; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 21. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 18. 2004.
