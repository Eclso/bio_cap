Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Shrubs ca. 4 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole sessile or subsessile (ca. 0.2 cm long), glabrous ; lamina 3.1-8.9 x 1.1-3.2 cm, ovate-lanceolate with tapering apex with obtuse tip, base rounded or subcordate , margin entire , coriaceous , glabrous ; midrib slightly canaliculate ; secondary_nerves obscure ; tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence long peduncled (2 cm long) umbels , subterminal and axillary ; flowers blue.
Fruit and Seed :Berry , 0.4-0.5 cm diameter, globose ; seed 1.

Ecology :

Occasional undergrowth in low elevation wet evergreen forests, up to 700 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- mostly in Central Sahyadri and also recorded in Agasthyamalai.

Literatures :

Hook. Kew Journ. 3: 121. 1851; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 505. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 182. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 2: 40. 1996.
