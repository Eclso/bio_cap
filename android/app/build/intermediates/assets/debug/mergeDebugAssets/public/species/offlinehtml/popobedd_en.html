Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Shrub to small trees , up to 10 m.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; petiole 0.1-0.2 cm long, semiterete ; lamina 4-8 x 1.5-2.5 cm, oblong-lanceolate , apex acute to gradually acuminate , base acute to rounded , chartaceous , granulate beneath; midrib and secondary_nerves impressed above; secondary_nerves 4-6 pairs, ascending; tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers extra-axillary or leaf opposed.
Fruit and Seed :Cluster of 1-seeded berries , globose or ovoid .

Ecology :

Understorey tree in evergreen forests between 700 and 1300 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Agasthyamalai Hills in South Sahyadri.

Literatures :

Hooker, Fl. Brit. Ind. 1: 68. 1872; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 17. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 20. 2004.
