Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; petiole 0.5-1 cm long, planoconvex in cross section, glabrous ; lamina 6.5-12.5 x 1.5-3 cm, linear lanceolate to linear elliptic , apex narrowly acute to gradually acuminate , base narrow acute , margin entire , glabrous , glaucous beneath; midrib slightly raised above; secondary_nerves 6-7 pairs, slender; tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence umbels in axillary and lateral sessile or shortly peduncled clusters; flowers unisexual .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , ellipsoid , seated on the enlarged fruiting_perianth_cup ; seed 1.

Ecology :

Understorey trees in low to high elevation evergreen forests between 200 and 1800 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :
