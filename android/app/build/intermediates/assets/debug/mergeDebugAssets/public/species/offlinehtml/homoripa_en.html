Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; stipules in pairs, caducous and leaving scar ; petiole to 1 cm; lamina to 16.5 × 2 cm, linear-lanceolate , apex acute , base cuneate , margin entire , subcoriaceous , glandular scales beneath; midrib raised above; secondary_nerves 15-19 pairs; tertiary_nerves reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence in axillary spikes ; flowers reddish, unisexual , dioecious .
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , 3-2 valved cocci ; seeds 3, ovoid .

Ecology :

On river beds.

Distribution :

Indomalaysia to Pacific Islands and South China; in the Western_Ghats- through out.
