Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 20 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 0.2-0.3 cm, canaliculate glabrous ; lamina 2.5-3.5 x 1.5-3 cm, obovate or suborbicular , apex shortly acuminate with blunt tip and sometimes obtuse , base acute , margin entire , glabrous , pellucid gland dotted, coriaceous ; midrib canaliculate above; intramarginal nerve present; secondary_nerves many, parallel ; tertiary_nerves admedially ramified .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence terminal corymbs , axillary or lateral ; flowers white. .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , oblong or obovoid , dark purple.

Ecology :

Canopy trees of high elevation evergreen forests between 1800 and 2200 m.

Distribution :

Western_Ghats and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- Agasthyamalai hills, Nilgiri and Palakkad hills.

Literatures :

Rep. 2: 180. 1843; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 480.1997 (re.ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 176. 2004.
