Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Evergreen trees up to 25 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; petiole 1-2 cm long, slightly canaliculate , subpubescent when young; lamina 11-26 x 4-10 cm, usually narrow oblong or oblong , sometimes lanceolate , apex abruptly or gradually acuminate , base usually rounded , sometimes slightly attenuate , margin entire , coriaceous , shinning above, glaucous beneath; midrib flat above; secondary_nerves 7-14 pairs, ascending; tertiary_nerves obscure , if visible laxly percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers unisexual , dioecious ; male flowers yellowish, in axillary panicles , fulvous pubescent ; female flowers larger in axillary racemes .
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , globose , to 3.2 cm across, glabrous ; seed one, globose , pale brown with laciniate aril .

Ecology :

Subcanopy trees in swampy evergreen forests up to 600 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Agasthyamalai (west) and Central Malanad.

Literatures :
