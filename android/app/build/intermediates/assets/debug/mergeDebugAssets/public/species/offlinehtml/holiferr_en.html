Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Large trees , up to 35 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral , clustered at twig ends; petiole 1-2 cm long, flat above with a pair of often caducous spurs ; lamina 10-27.5 x 2.5-8.5 cm oblanceolate , apex shortly acuminate to obtuse or rounded , base cuneate to decurrent , margin revolute , coriaceous , glabrous , drying brown beneath; midrib raised above; secondary_nerves 9-14 pairs, gradually curved; tertiary_nerves reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence terminal panicle , dark purple-brown tomentose ; flowers polygamous .
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , obovoid , wholly enclosed in hypocarp , 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Canopy trees in low and medium wet evergreen forests up to 1300 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to Western_Ghats , rare in South Sahyadri and frequent in Central Sahyadri.
