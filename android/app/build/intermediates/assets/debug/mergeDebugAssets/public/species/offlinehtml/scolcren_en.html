Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 18 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; stipule caducous ; petiole 0.6-1.3 cm long, glabrous ; lamina 6-11.5 x1.5-5 cm, elliptic to lanceolate , apex gradually acuminate , base acute or cuneate , slightly asymmetric , crenate-serrate at margin, coriaceous , glabrous ; midrib flat above; secondary_nerves 6-8 pairs, ascending, lower most two pairs closer and oblique ; tertiary_nerves reticulo-percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in axillary racemes , white with many stamens .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , globose , apiculate , to 2 cm across; seeds 2-6, semilunar .

Ecology :

Subcanopy trees in evergreen to semi-evergreen forests up to 1800 m.

Distribution :

Indomalaysia; in the Western_Ghats- South, Central and south Maharashtra Sahyadris.

Literatures :

Ann. Sci. Nat. Bot. ser. 4.8: 250. 1857; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 52. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 33. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 1: 273. 1996.
