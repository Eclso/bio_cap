Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Large shrubs or treelets ca. 4 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 0.2 cm long, glabrous ; lamina 6.5-11.5 x 1.5-2.8 cm, lanceolate with narrowly tapering apex , base rounded , margin entire , coriaceous , glabrous ; midrib canaliculate with intramarginal_nerve ; secondary_nerves faintly visible at least when dry, ca. 15 pairs; tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence clusters of short peduncled cymes , lateral ; petals blue; calyx red.
Fruit and Seed :Berry , black; seed 1.

Ecology :

Rare undergrowth in low elevation evergreen forests, up to 800 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- South Sahyadri and North Malabar.
