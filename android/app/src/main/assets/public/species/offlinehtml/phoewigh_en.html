Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 15 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; petiole to 2 cm long, planoconvex in cross section, tomentose ; lamina to 6.5-11.5 x 3.5-5.8 cm, generally obovate or sometimes elliptic-oblanceolate , apex acuminate , base cuneate , tomentose beneath at least when young; midrib slightly raised above; secondary_nerves ca. 6 pairs, strong; tertiary_nerves distantly percurrent , strong.
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary slender panicles , tomentose .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , globose , 1 cm across; seed 1.

Ecology :

Canopy trees in high elevation evergreen forests, between 1400 and 2300 m.

Distribution :

India and Myanmar; in the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :

DC, Prodr. 15: 38. 1864; Gamble, Fl. Madras 2: 1228. 1993 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 401. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 1: 72. 1996.
