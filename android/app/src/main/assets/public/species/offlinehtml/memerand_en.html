Tamil: Kanyava, Malamthetti, Perungaca, Vacci Malayalam: Kannavu, Kanyavu, Kashavu, Kashava, Kayavu Kannada: Doddanekkare, Lokundi, Mundi

Synonyms :

Memecylon malabaricum (Cl.) Cogn.; Memecylon amplexicaule Roxb. var. malabarica Cl.

Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate , sessile or subsessile ; lamina 5-13 x 2.5-5.5 cm, ovate to ovate-lanceolate , apex acute or obtuse , base rounded to subcordate , margin entire , thickly coriaceous ; midrib canaliculate above; secondary_nerves and tertiary_nerves obscure or former slightly visible when dry.
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers blue, fascicled , singly or in very short pedunculate cymes , generally on lateral tubercles .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , globose , seed 1.

Ecology :

Common understorey trees in evergreen forests up to 2400 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :
