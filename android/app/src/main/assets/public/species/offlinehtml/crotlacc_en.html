Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 10 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; petiole 0.8-3 cm long, stellate hairy ; lamina 3.8-13 x 2-6 cm, broadly ovate , apex acute to acuminate , base subcordate , irregularly serrate or crenate , stellate hairy on both surfaces, trinerved at base; secondary_nerves 4-6 pairs; tertiary_nerves strongly reticulate ; stipitate glands 2 pairs, at the junction of lamina and petiole beneath.
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence racemes with male flowers towards apex and female flowers at base.
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , 0.8-1 cm diameter, subglobose , white stellate hairy ; seeds 3.

Ecology :

Understorey trees in high elevation evergreen forests, between 1300 and 2300 m.

Distribution :

Peninsular India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :
