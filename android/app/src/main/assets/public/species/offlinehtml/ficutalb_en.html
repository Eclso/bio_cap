Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Evergreen tree , up to 30 m tall, with aerial roots , usually epiphytic when young.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; stipules 0.5 cm long, ovate , pubescent , caducous and leaving annular scar ; petioles 1-3 cm long, canaliculate , pubescent when young; lamina 5- 12.7 x 1.2-6.4 cm, ovate or elliptic , apex caudate - acuminate to caudate with blunt tip, base cuneate-acute , margin entire , coriaceous , shining above; midrib raised above; secondary_nerves 8-10 pairs, looped near margin; tertiary_nerves admedially ramified .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence syconia, sessile , in axillary pairs, depressed globose ; flowers unisexual .
Fruit and Seed :Syconium globose , 0.5-0.6 cm across.

Ecology :

Occasional in evergreen forests up to 1000 m.

Distribution :

India, China and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- South, Central and Maharashtra Sahyadris.
