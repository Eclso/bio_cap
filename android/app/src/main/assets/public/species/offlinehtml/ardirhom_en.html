Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; petiole 0.4-0.8 cm long, slender, glabrous when mature; lamina 2.3-6 x 1.3-2.8 cm, rhomboid or elliptic-oblanceolate , apex acute to acuminate with blunt tip, base attenuate or cuneate , margin entire or obscurely crenulate , recurved , glabrous , chartaceous ; midrib slightly canaliculate above; secondary_nerves and tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary umbellate cymes ; flowers pinkish-white; peduncle 1-1.5 cm long; pedicel 0.6-0.8 cm long, slender.
Fruit and Seed :Berry , ca. 0.6 cm across, with persistent stylar base.

Ecology :

Common understorey trees in high elevation evergreen forests usually between 1400 and 1800 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Agasthyamalai, Anaimalai and Nilgiris.
