Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees , up to 8 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 0.7-1.5 cm long, canaliculate above, peltate_scaly ; lamina 4-10 x 2-4.5 cm, usually elliptic sometimes narrow obovate , apex acute to acuminate , base acute , margin revolute , coriaceous , peltate_scaly beneath; midrib canaliculate above; secondary_nerves 5-8 pairs, slightly impressed above; tertiary_nerves obscurely reticulo-percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence compound umbel and corymbiform ; flower white.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , oblong-ellipsoid , peltate_scaly , 1 cm long; 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Understorey tree in medium and high altitude evergreen forests between 900-2400 m.

Distribution :

Indomalaysia; in the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :

Don, Prodr. Fl. Nep.142. 1825; Keshava Murthy and Yoganarasimhan, Fl. Coorg (Kodagu) 213. 1990; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 575. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 209. 2004.
