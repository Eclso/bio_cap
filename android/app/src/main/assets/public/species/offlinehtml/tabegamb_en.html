Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small tree or shrubs , up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , pairs unequal, decussate ; petiole 1 cm long with a short clasping sheath at base; lamina 6.5-12 x 2.4- 4.5 cm, elliptic or elliptic-lanceolate , apex caudate - acuminate , acumen to 2 cm long, base acute , glabrous , pale beneath; midrib raised above; secondary_nerves 7-9 pairs; tertiary_nerves reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers bisexual , in terminal corymbose cymes , white.
Fruit and Seed :Follicles 4 x 1 cm, oblong , apex caudate ; Seeds many, arillate .

Ecology :

Understorey trees or shrubs , very common in medium elevation wet evergreen forests between 600 and 1400 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- South Sahyadri.

Literatures :

Bull. Bot. Sur. India 12: 1. 1970; Gamble, Fl. Madras 2: 813. 1993 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 285-286. 2004.
