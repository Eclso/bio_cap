Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral , usually crowded at apex ; petiole 0.5-1.2 cm long, canaliculate , glabrous ; lamina 2.5-10 x 1.3-5 cm, elliptic or obovate , apex acute or subacuminate , sometimes obtuse , base acute , margin entire to crenulate ; chartaceous or thinly coriaceous , glabrous ; midrib canaliculate ; secondary_nerves 5-8 pairs thin, branched; tertiary_nerves finely reticulate , sometimes obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence few flowered racemes ; flowers yellow; pedicel up to 1 cm long.
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , obliquely 2-valved , ca. 1 cm across; pedicel filiform ; seeds 4-8.

Ecology :

Trees along margins of mediuma and high elevation evergreen forests between 1200 m and 2300 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the southern Western_Ghats- South Sahyadri and Bababudangiri hills in Central Sahyadri.

Literatures :
