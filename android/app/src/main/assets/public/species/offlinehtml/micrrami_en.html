Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees , up to 8 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole about 0.3-0.4 cm long; lamina 6.5-8.5 x 3.5-4.7 cm, broadly elliptic or obovate , apex rounded , sometimes with retuse tip, base rounded or cuneate , margin revolute , coriaceous , pale beneath; secondary_nerves 6-8 pairs, not prominent above; tertiary_nerves admedially_ramified .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in sessile clusters at axils or on older branches .
Fruit and Seed :Capsule ellipsoid with persistent style , 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Common canopy trees in high altitude evergreen forests between 1800 and 2400 m.

Distribution :

India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- common in Nilgiris, Anaimalai and Palani Hills.
