Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Large trees , up to 35 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral , clustered at twig ends; petiole 5-8 cm long, stout, terete , swollen at base, glabrous ; lamina 29-60 x11-17 cm, wide obovate , apex rounded , base acute , margin revolute , coriaceous , glabrous ; midrib flat above, stout beneath; secondary_nerves 14-18 pairs, straight, prominent, raised above, curving at margin; tertiary_nerves reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary and terminal panicle , glabrous ; flowers polygamous .
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , obliquely oblong , black up to 3 cm long, seated on fleshy hypocarp , 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Canopy trees generally in medium elevation wet evergreen forests at 600-1200 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to Western_Ghats ; south of Palghat gap in South Sahyadri.

Literatures :

Fl. Sylv. 2: 232. 1870; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 267. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 113. 2004.
