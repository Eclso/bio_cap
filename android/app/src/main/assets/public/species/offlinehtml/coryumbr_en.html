Corypha umbraculifera L. - ARECACEAE

Vernacular names :

Tamil: Talippanai, Kodaipannai. Malayalam: Kodapanna, Talippana. English: Talipot palm.

Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Palms up to 20 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves compound , palmate , circular or sublunate ; rachis stout, concave, spinous at the edges; lamina plicate , cleft to about the middle into 80-100 linear lanceolate bifid lobes .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence spadices , terminal erect, paniculate , pyramidal, spathes many, tubular, flowers small, bisexual .
Fruit and Seed :Drupe ; 1-seeded , albumen not ruminate .

Ecology :

In the semi-evergreen forests, up to 600 m.

Distribution :

Peninsular India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- wildly found in Yana, North Kanara (Uttara Kannada).

Literatures :

Sp. Pl. (ed. 2) 1657. 1763; Gamble, Fl. Madras 3: 1561.1998 (re. ed); Cook, Fl. Bombay 1: 808. 1902; Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 506. 2004.
