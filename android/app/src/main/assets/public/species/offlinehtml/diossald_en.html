Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 10 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; petiole 0.2-0.4 cm long, stout, densely hirsute ; lamina 5-14 x 2.2-6 cm oblong or oblanceolate , apex bluntly acute to acuminate , base rounded to subcordate , subcoriaceous , densely soft hairy on both surface when young, later become glabrous above except on midrib ; secondary_nerves 6-11 pairs; tertiary_nerves broadly reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers unisexual ; female flowers clusters of 1-3, axillary .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , conical or ellipsoid-ovate with dense rufous bristles ; fruiting_calyx_lobes oblong ; seeds 3-4.

Ecology :

Understorey tree in evergreen forests, up to 900 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- mostly in Central Sahyadri.
