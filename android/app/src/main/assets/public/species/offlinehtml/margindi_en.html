Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Deciduous trees , up to 25 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; stipules lateral , lanceolate ; petiole 0.5-0.8 cm long, planoconvex in cross section, glabrous ; lamina 5.3-13 × 3.5-6 cm, elliptic , elliptic-ovate to obovate , apex acuminate with blunt tip, base asymmetric , margin entire , shining above, glaucous beneath, glabrous , membranous ; midrib slightly raised above; secondary_nerves 6-10 pairs, slender; tertiary_nerves broadly reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers unisexual , dioecious , green; male flowers fascicled , axillary , pedicels ca. 0.5 cm long; female flowers solitary or few together, pedicels 1-2 cm long.
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , 0.8 cm across, depressed globose , 3-lobed ; seeds 3, subtrigonous; arillate .

Ecology :

Canopy trees in semi-evergreen to evergreen forests up to 1200 m.

Distribution :

Indomalaysia to Australia; in the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :
