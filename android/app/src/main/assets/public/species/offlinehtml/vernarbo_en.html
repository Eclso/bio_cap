Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Medium sized trees , up to 15 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves alternate , spiral , clustered at twig ends; petiole 0.6-2.6 cm long, subterete , tomentose ; lamina 8.5-19 x 4-11 cm, obovate or broadly-obovate , apex acute or abruptly, shortly acuminate , base cuneate to acute , margin entire or sometimes distantly double serrate with alternate small and large tooth , chartaceous , tomentose beneath; midrib tomentose above; secondary_nerves 10-15 pairs, looped near margin; tertiary_nerves reticulo-percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in heads of large terminal panicled cymes , purple.
Fruit and Seed :Achene , ribbed ; 1-seeded , pappus uniseriate .

Ecology :

Occasional along margins of evergreen forests, up to 1900 m.

Distribution :

Indomalaysia; in the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :

Trans. Linn. Soc. London 14: 218.1824; Gamble, Fl. Madras 2: 672.1993 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 257. 2004; Keshava Murthy and Yoganarasimhan, Fl. Coorg (Kodagu) 256. 1990; Cook, Fl. Bombay 2: 12. 1902.
