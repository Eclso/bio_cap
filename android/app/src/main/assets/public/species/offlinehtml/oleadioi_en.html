Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 15 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 0.6-1.3 cm long, canaliculate ; lamina 7.5-17.5 x 2.3-7.5 cm, elliptic to elliptic-oblong , apex gradually acuminate to subacute , base acute or attenuate , margin distantly serrate (with strong teeth ) or entire , coriaceous to subcoriaceous , glabrous ; midrib flat above, usually reddish when dry; secondary_nerves 8-12 pairs; tertiary and higher order nerves obscure or slightly impressed .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary divaricate panicles ; flowers polygamodioecious , cream-white; pedicel 0.4 cm long.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , ellipsoid , blue when ripe; seed one.

Ecology :

Understorey trees in open evergreen to semi-evergreen and moist deciduous forests up to 1200 m.

Distribution :

India; in the Western_Ghats- throughout.
