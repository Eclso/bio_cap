Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees , up to 8 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves compound , pinnate , alternate , spiral , stipulate ; leaflets 8-11, lamina 7-10 x 3-4 cm, ovate-oblong , apex caudate - acuminate , base rounded , asymmetric , entire , coriaceous ; secondary_nerves ca. 10 pairs, slender; tertiary_nerves broadly reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence panicled umbels ; flowers green.
Fruit and Seed :Berry 5-angled, subglobose , pyrenes 5-8; seeds compressed.

Ecology :

Rare in high elevation evergreen forests, between 1500 and 1800 m.

Distribution :

India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- Nilgiris, Anaimalai and Agasthyamalai Hills.

Literatures :

J. Bot. London 3: 181. 1865; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 568. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 206. 2004.
