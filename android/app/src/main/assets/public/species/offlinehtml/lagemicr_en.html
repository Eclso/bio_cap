Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Large deciduous tree , up to 25 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple opposite to subopposite , sometimes upper leaves alternate ; petiole 0.5 cm, planoconvex in cross section, minutely puberulous ; lamina 11 x 4.5 cm, elliptic , apex acute to acuminate , base acute , glabrous , white glaucous beneath; midrib slightly canaliculate ; secondary_nerves 8-10 pairs; tertiary_nerves closely_horizontally_percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in axillary panicles ; petals white.
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , ellipsoid , woody , 4-valved , loculicidal , 1 cm long; seeds numerous , flat, erect, falcately winged at the apex .

Ecology :

Common in deciduous and semi-evergreen forests and found occasionally along margins and opening of evergreen forests, up to 1000 m,.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- through out.
