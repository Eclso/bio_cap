Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Large trees up to 30 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate distichous ; petiole 0.8-2.2 cm long, canaliculate , usually upper half stouter, glabrous ; lamina 8-18 x 4-6.5 cm, usually ovate to oblong-ovate , apex slightly acuminate to acute or obtuse , base rounded to slightly attenuate , margin entire , subcoriaceous , glabrous beneath; midrib slightly raised above; secondary_nerves 4-6 pairs, ascending; tertiary_nerves slender, horizontally reticulo-percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary cymes or clusters on short tubercles ; flowers subsessile , greenish-white.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , club shaped when young later globose , deep purple when ripe; seed one.

Ecology :

Canopy trees in wet evergreen forests up to 700 m.

Distribution :

Indomalaysia; in the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.
