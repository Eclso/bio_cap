Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 8 m tall.
Leaves :Crown of fronds at the top of the trunk ; fronds bipinnate , 200 cm long; stipes ca. 100 cm long, with stout base, long brown hairy throughout; pinnae ca. 12 pairs, alternate , distinctly petiolate ; pinnules 7.5-12 x 1.5-2.5 cm, pinnatifid , lobes alternate , oblong , 0.7-1.5 x 0.2-0.4 cm, margin crenate , membranous ; nerves forked.
Inflorescence / Flower :N/A
Fruit and Seed :Spore producing structures ‘sori’ spherical seated on nerves forks.

Ecology :

Along streams between 1000 and 2200 m.

Distribution :

South Sahyadri in the Western_Ghats .
