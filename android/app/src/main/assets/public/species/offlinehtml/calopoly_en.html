Calophyllum polyanthum Wall. Ex Choisy - CLUSIACEAE

Vernacular names :

Tamil: Katta pinnai, Punnai, Srikonnai. Malayalam: Kattupunna, Malampunna, Pinnapai, Punna, Punnapine. Kannada: Poon, Salhonne, Surhoni.

Synonyms :

Calophyllum elatum Bedd.

Images :

Languages:

Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Tall trees , up to 35 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 0.8-2.5 cm long, planoconvex in cross section, glabrescent ; lamina 5-9 x 2-4 cm, elliptic-lanceolate , apex acute to acuminate , base usually attenuate , sometimes acute , coriaceous ; secondary_nerves many, close, parallel , nearly right angle to midrib , terminating at thick margin.
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers white, fragrant, in panicles .
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , ellipsoid , apiculate , to 3.7 cm long, 1- seeded .

Ecology :

Common canopy trees in wet evergreen forests up to 1600 m.

Distribution :

Indochina and Indomalaysia; in the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :

Descr. Guttif. Ind. 43. 1849; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 76. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 40. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 1: 203. 1996.
