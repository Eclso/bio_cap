Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small deciduous trees , up to 10 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves compound , imparipinnate , clustered at twig ends; rachis 15-30 cm long, terete , striate , pulvinus , glabrous ; leaflets opposite , 3-7 pairs with terminal one, petiolule to 0.2 cm long; leaflet lamina 3-8 x 1.4-3 cm, narrow oblong sometimes oblong-ovate , apex acuminate , base asymmetricaly rounded , margin crenulate with shallow crenature , chartaceous , glabrous ; midrib slightly raised above, with intramarginal_nerve ; secondary_nerves 8-10; tertiary_nerves admedially_ramified .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence terminal panicle ; flowers bisexual , white, fragrant.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , small, oblong , 0.8 x 0.4 cm, 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Along rivulets amidst evergreen and moist deciduous forests up to 1000 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to Western_Ghats ; South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :

Prodr. 1: 172. 1834; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 262. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 113. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 2: 209. 1996.
