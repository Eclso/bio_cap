Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees or large shrubs , up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; petioles 0.2-0.5 cm long; lamina 5-6.5 x 2.5-3.5 cm, ovate-elliptic , apex long acuminate , base cuneate , glabrous except midrib beneath; secondary_nerves ca. 6 pairs, slender; tertiary_nerves reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers creamy white, axillary , solitary or 2-3 together; peduncles strigose ; pedicels very short, slender, pubescent .
Fruit and Seed :Clustered 1-2 seeded berries , pisiform .

Ecology :

Understorey tree in medium elevation wet evergreen forests, between 600 and 1000 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- rare in South Sahyadri (Agasthyamalai and Anaimalai Hills).

Literatures :

Trans. Linn. Soc. Lond. 20: 5. 1846; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 24. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 19. 2004.
