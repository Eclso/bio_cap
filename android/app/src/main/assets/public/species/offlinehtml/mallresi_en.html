Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple with opposite , decussate , unequal pairs; stipules caducous ; petiole 0.5 to 1 cm long, canaliculate , swollen at both ends; lamina 5.5-16 x 2.7-4.5 cm (variable) rhombic-elliptic to oblanceolate , narrowed at both ends, apex acute to acuminate , base cuneate , margin entire to sinuate-dentate , resinous yellow glandular beneath; midrib slightly raised above; secondary_nerves ca. 7 pairs; tertiary_nerves obliquely_percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers unisexual , in axillary spikes .
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , 3 cocci , sparsely yellow glandular and muricate ; seeds 1 per cell.

Ecology :

Understorey trees in evergreen forests up to 1300 m.

Distribution :

Peninsular India; in the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :
