Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 15 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; stipule caducous ; petiole 0.4-0.9 cm long, canaliculate , glabrous ; lamina 5-15 x 2.5-6.4 cm, narrow elliptic to elliptic-ovate , apex acuminate , acumen usually twisted, base attenuate or subacute , margin entire or serrate , chartaceous , glabrous , with pellucid transparent dots and lines; midrib flat above; secondary_nerves 6-10 pairs; tertiary_nerves broadly reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in axillary fascicles , glabrous , white.
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , 2-3 valved , subglobose to ellipsoid , orange when ripe; seeds many with arils.

Ecology :

Subcanopy trees in evergreen forests up to 1800 m.

Distribution :

India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.
