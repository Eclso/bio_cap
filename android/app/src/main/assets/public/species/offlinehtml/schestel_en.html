Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees or apparently straggling shrubs .
Leaves :Leaves compound , digitate , alternate , spiral , pulvinate , stipule adnate to the petiole ; petioles 6-20 cm long, sheathing at base; petiolule 2-6 cm long; leaflets 3-6, usually 5, lamina 4.2-10 x 3.5-6.4 cm, obovate , sometimes oblique , apex rounded to slightly obtuse or emarginate , base attenuate , glabrous , shining above, margin recurved ; midrib impressed above; secondary_nerves 4-6 pairs, looped ; tertiary_nerves broadly reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in umbels arranged in panicled racemes , 5-8 per umbel .
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , subglobose , 0.4-0.5 cm diameter; style tip persistent in fruits ; seeds compressed.

Ecology :

Along streams or in open evergreen forests up to 2000 m.

Distribution :

Peninsular India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.
