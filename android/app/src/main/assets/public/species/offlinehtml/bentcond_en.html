Vernacular names :

Tamil: Kantha panai, Varei kamugu, Varukamuvu. Malayalam: Kanthakamugu, Kantal, Kanthal. English: Hill Areca nut.

Synonyms :

Bentickia coddapanna Berry

Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Palm 15 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves compound , pinnate , 1.5-2 m long; leaflets 60-75 x 2.5-5 cm, linear oblong , 2-3 united, tip usually bifid ; lobes 5-10 cm.
Inflorescence / Flower :Spadix from axil of fallen leaves just below crown. Spathe many, membranous , flowers small, sunken in branches of spadix ; male flowers scarlet; female flowers lilac or violet, peduncles branched.
Fruit and Seed :Fruit subglobose , rather compressed, 1-1.2 cm diameter, red, 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Locally common on rocky cliffs between 1000 and 1900 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- South Sahyadri, (Agasthyamalai, Elamalai, Palani Hills).
