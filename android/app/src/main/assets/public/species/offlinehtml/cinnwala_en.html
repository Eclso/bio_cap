Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Tree up to 6 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite to subopposite ; petiole 0.5-1.5 cm, planoconvex in cross section, glabrous ; lamina 7-11 x 2-4 cm, elliptic-oblong to lanceolate , apex acuminate , base acute , margin entire , chartaceous ; trinerved , basal ; lateral nerves not reaching the leaf tip, tertiary_nerves horizontally_percurrent ; higher order nerves closely reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers axillary panicled cymes , few flowered, glabrous .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , ellipsoid , apiculate ; fruiting_perianth_cup shallow with persistent lobes ; seed 1.

Ecology :

Rare understorey trees in evergreen forests ca. 1100 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Agasthyamalai Hills.
