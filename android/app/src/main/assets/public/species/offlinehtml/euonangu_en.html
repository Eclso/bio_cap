Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees , up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; stipules caducous ; petiole 0.4-0.7 cm long, canaliculate , glabrous ; lamina 5-10 x 1.3-4 cm, narrow elliptic or lanceolate , apex narrowly acute or acuminate , base acute ; margin thick, slightly incurved, glabrous ; secondary_nerves obscure beneath, slightly visible above; tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary cymes ; flowers dull purple.
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , obovate , 5 winged , 1-2 seeds per lobe .

Ecology :

Understorey tree in medium elevation evergreen forests, between 800 and 1400 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- locally frequent in Palakkad hills of Tamil Nadu Ghats and rare in South Sahyadri.

Literatures :

Wight, Ic. t. 1053. 1846; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 203.1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 96. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 2: 95. 1996.
