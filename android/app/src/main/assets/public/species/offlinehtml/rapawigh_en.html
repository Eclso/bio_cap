Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees up to 10 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral , subverticillate ; petiole 0.5-1 cm long, planoconvex in cross section, glabrous ; lamina 4-8 x 1-2.3 cm, elliptic-oblanceolate , apex obtuse , base attenuate , margin entire , transparent gland dotted and in lines, glabrous , coriaceous ; midrib flat above; secondary_nerves and tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence sessile or shortly pedicelled umbels ; flowers polygamous .
Fruit and Seed :Berry globose with persistent style ; seed one.

Ecology :

Trees along margin of the high elevation evergreen forests between 1600 and 2200 m. .

Distribution :

South India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- South Sahyadri and Nilgiri, and rare in Central Sahyadri.
