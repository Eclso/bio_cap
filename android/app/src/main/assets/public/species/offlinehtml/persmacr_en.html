Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 30 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral , clustered at twig ends; petiole 1.2-4.5 cm long, canaliculate , glabrous ; lamina 7-20.3 x 3-8 cm long, variable in shape, obovate or elliptic to elliptic-oblong , apex rounded or acuminate or acute , base acute to rounded , margin entire , coriaceous , glabrous , glaucous beneath, aromatic when squeeze; midrib canaliculate above; secondary_nerves 8-12 pairs; tertiary_nerves distantly percurrent , slender; higher order nerves closely reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in terminal , spreading panicles .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , globose to 2 cm across, black when ripe.

Ecology :

Canopy trees in evergreen forests up to 2000 m.

Distribution :

Peninsular India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- South, Central and south Maharashtra Sahyadris.

Literatures :

Reinwardtia 6: 193. 1962; Gamble, Fl. Madras 2: 1227. 1993 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 401. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 1: 71. 1996.
