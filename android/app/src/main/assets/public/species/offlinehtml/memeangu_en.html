Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Large spreading shrubs or treelets ca. 4 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 0.3-0.4 cm long, planoconvex in cross section, glabrous ; lamina 3-9.5 x 0.5-1.9 cm, narrow linear-elliptic to linear-lanceolate , apex acute , base attenuate , margin entire , glabrous , coriaceous ; midrib slightly canaliculate above with intramarginal_nerve ; secondary and tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers axillary umbels , blue.
Fruit and Seed :Berry , globose , black-purple, 0.7 cm across; seed 1.

Ecology :

Along river and stream banks in evergreen forests at very low altitudes up to 300 m.

Distribution :

South India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- rare in South Sahyadri.

Literatures :
