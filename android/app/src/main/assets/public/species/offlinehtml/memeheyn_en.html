Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees ca. 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 0.7-1 cm long, stout, planoconvex in cross section, glabrous ; lamina 13-16 x 4-5 cm, lanceolate , tapering into acuminate apex with blunt tip, base acute , margin entire , coriaceous , glabrous ; midrib slightly raised above; secondary_nerves ca. 20 pairs, distantly parallel and joining with intramarginal_nerve , tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers nearly sessile , in axillary and lateral fascicles , purple; disk rays prominent.
Fruit and Seed :Berry , seed 1.

Ecology :

Rare undergrowth mostly in low elevation evergreen forests up to 900 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- occasional in South Sahyadri and southern part of Central Sahyadris (up to Coorg Region).
