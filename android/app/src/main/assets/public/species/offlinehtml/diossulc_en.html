Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 8 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; petiole to 2 cm long, terete ; lamina 13-20 x 4-7 cm, elliptic to oblong-lanceolate , apex acuminate , base attenuate , glabrous , chartaceous ; secondary_nerves ca. 15 pairs, slender; tertiary_nerves broadly reticulate , not prominent.
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers unisexual ; male flowers , sessile axillary clusters; female flower clusters of 1-5, axillary or on older branches , sessile .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , sessile , ovoid with truncate apex , 8-10 grooved , hairy ; fruiting_calyx_lobes with wavy margin; seeds 10.

Ecology :

Understorey tree in wet evergreen forests, up to 250 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- recorded along river in the west of Periyar Regions (South Sahyadri).
