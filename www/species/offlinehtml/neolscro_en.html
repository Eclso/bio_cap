Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 16 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; petiole 3 (-6) cm long, planoconvex in cross section, glabrous ; lamina 7.5-13 x 3.5-5.5 cm elliptic-ovate or broadly elliptic , apex acute-acuminate , base acute - attenuate , glabrous , glaucous beneath, scrobiculate above, coriaceous ; trinerved just above the base, with ca. 3 pairs of secondary_nerves ; tertiary_nerves distantly percurrent ; higher order nerves closely reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence umbels , axillary or lateral clusters.
Fruit and Seed :Berry , globose , to 1 cm across; fruiting_perianth cupular ; seed 1.

Ecology :

Occasional trees in evergreen forests between 1000 and 2400 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- South Sahyadri and Nilgiris.
