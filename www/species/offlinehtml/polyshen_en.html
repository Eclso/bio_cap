Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees , up to 20 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; petioles ca. 0.5 cm long; lamina 15-26 x 6-8.5 cm, oblong or elliptic-lanceolate , apex acuminate , acumen to 3 cm long, base rounded or truncate , glabrous , midrib impressed above, secondary_nerves 10-14 pairs, tertiary_nerves reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers on twigs solitary and/or up to 10 in fascicles on tubercles of the trunk ; pedicels 4-5.5 cm long and 2-3 mm across.
Fruit and Seed :Cluster of berries , up to 7, oblong-ovoid , glabrous , 3 x 2.5 cm, stalks ca. 1.8 cm long; seeds oblong .

Ecology :

Rare in wet evergreen forests between 600 and 1000 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Recorded only in Shendurni, Agasthyamalai Hills.
