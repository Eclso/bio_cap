Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees up to 7 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral , clustered at twig ends; petiole 0.5-0.7 cm long, planoconvex in cross section, glabrous ; lamina 5-12 x 1-4.5 cm, obovate , apex rounded or retuse , base attenuate , margin entire , transparent gland dotted and in lines, glabrous , coriaceous ; midrib flat above; secondary_nerves ca. 14 pairs, not prominent; tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence umbels , sessile , axillary or on old branchlets ; flowers bisexual ; pedicel 1.5 cm long.
Fruit and Seed :Berry , globose , purple; seed one.

Ecology :

Understorey trees in high elevation evergreen forests between 1600 and 2200 m. .

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Anaimalai and Palni hills.
