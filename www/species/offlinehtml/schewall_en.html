Schefflera wallichiana (Wt. & Arn.) Harms - ARALIACEAE

Vernacular names Images Botanical descriptions Ecology Distribution Literatures

Vernacular names :

Malayalam: Kannimaram, Modakom. Kannada: Beddhele, Bethu mara, Maruli.

Synonyms :

Paratropia wallichiana Wt. & Arn.; Heptapleurum wallichianum Cl.

Images :

Languages:

Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees .
Leaves :Leaves compound , digitate , alternate , spiral , pulvinate , stipule adnate to the petiole , sheath at base; petiolule to 5 cm, puberulous ; leaflets 5-8, lamina 9-20 x 3.3-7 cm, oblong or obovate , apex acuminate , base truncate or acute , coriaceous ; secondary_nerves 12-14 pairs; tertiary_nerves ramified .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence umbels in large panicles , terminal , 18 cm long.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe ; seeds compressed.

Ecology :

In evergreen forests between 700 and 2000 m.

Distribution :

Peninsular India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :

Engler & Prantl, Naturl Pflanzenfam. 3 (8): 38. 1894; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 570. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 208. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 2: 275. 1996. Keshava Murthy and Yoganarasimhan, Fl. Coorg (Kodagu) 212. 1990.
