Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 15 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves compound , paripinnate ; stipules ovate , appendages small, falcate ; rachis of leaves flattened and winged ; leaflets 3-4 pairs, lamina 7.6-10.2 x 2.5-3.8 cm, lanceolate-oblanceolate , apex gradually long acuminate to caudate , base asymmetric , chartaceous ; secondary_nerves ca. 10 pairs, upper pairs looped ; tertiary_nerves reticulo-percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence cauliflorus fascicled racemes , tawny-velvety ; petals white, veined with pink; calyx crimson red.
Fruit and Seed :Pod scarlet, pubescent .

Ecology :

Understorey tree in medium elevation wet evergreen forests between 900 and 1000 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Agasthyamalai and Elamalai Hills in South Sahyadri.

Literatures :
