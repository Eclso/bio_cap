Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 10 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; stipules caducous ; petiole 0.7-2.2 cm long, subterete , pubescent ; lamina 8-23 x 3.5-10 cm, usually oblong to elliptic-oblong , apex caudate - acuminate , base acute or cuneate , margin serrate , chartaceous , glabrous ; midrib raised above; secondary_nerves 5-7 pairs, obliquely ascending towards apex ; tertiary_nerves closely_horizontally_percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in axillary short cymes or solitary , tomentose ; petals white.
Fruit and Seed :Berry , woody , globose , apiculate 6 (-10) cm across usually brown tomentose , black when young; seeds numerous .

Ecology :

Common understorey trees in wet evergreen forests and along streams, generally up to 1000 m sometimes extends up to 1700 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- very common in South and Central Sahyadris.
