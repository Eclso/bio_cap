Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Large shrubs ca. 4 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petioles 0.4 cm, planoconvex in cross section, glabrous ; lamina 6-10 x 2-3 cm, lanceolate , apex gradually acuminate , base acute , margin entire ; midrib slightly canaliculate ; secondary_nerves obscurely visible, parallel and joining into intramarginal_nerve ; tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in axillary or lateral fascicles , few flowered, sessile ; disk rays faint.
Fruit and Seed :Berry , globose , yellow; seed 1.

Ecology :

Undergrowth in medium elevation evergreen forests between 600 and 900 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- occasional in South Sahyadri, Palakkad to Wayanad in Central Sahyadri.

Literatures :

Bull. Misc. Inform. Kew 1919. 226. 1919; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 503. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 182. 2004.
