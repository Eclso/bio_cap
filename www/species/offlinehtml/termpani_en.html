Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Large trees , up to 20 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves usually subopposite or upper ones alternate ; petiole 0.6 - 1.7 cm long, subterete , subglabrous ; lamina 9-18 x 4.5-7 cm, oblong or ovate-oblong , apex acuminate , base rounded to cordate , coriaceous , slightly silky pubescent on both surface, glabrous when mature, coriaceous , black above when dry; midrib thinly raised above; secondary_nerves 10-15 pairs, gradually curved; tertiary_nerves slender reticulo-percurrent ; sessile glands in pair at the junction of petiole and midrib beneath.
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence panicled spikes ; flowers pale white.
Fruit and Seed :Samara , brownish red, unequally winged , middle wing larger, 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Along margin or in the openings of evergreen and semi-evergreen forests, up to 1200 m.

Distribution :

Peninsular India; in the Western_Ghats- throughout.
