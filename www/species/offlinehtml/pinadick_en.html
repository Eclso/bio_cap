Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Slender monoecious palm , with annular leaf_scars , up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves compound , pinnate , to 1.5 m long; sheath to 30 cm long; leaflets to 50 x 3 cm, linear-lanceolate , opposite , apex premorse , glabrous .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence to 35 cm long.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , ellipsoid ; seed ovoid or ellipsoid , ruminate .

Ecology :

Locally common in moist pockets in evergreen forests.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :
