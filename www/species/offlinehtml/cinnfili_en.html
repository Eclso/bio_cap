Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees 10 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite-subopposite ; petiole 0.7-1.5 cm long, slender, glabrous , canaliculate ; lamina 5-8.5 x 2-5 cm, linear-lanceolate to elliptic-ovate , apex acuminate to caudate - acuminate with blunt tip, acumen 1-3 cm, base acute to attenuate , subcoriaceous , glabrous , glaucous beneath; trinerved , suprabased , lateral nerves reaching not reaching the apex ; tertiary_nerves horizontally_percurrent ; higher order nerve closely reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary or pseudoterminal panicles , slender, glabrous , few flowered; pedicels filiform .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , ellipsoid , 0.8 cm long; fruiting_perianth_cup shallow; seed 1.

Ecology :

Understorey trees in medium elevation wet evergreen forests, between 700 and 1500 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Agasthyamalai Hills.

Literatures :

Bull. Bot. Surv. India 25. 92. 1983; Gamble, Fl. Madras 2: 1223. 1993 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 396. 2004.
