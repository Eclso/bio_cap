Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees up to 8 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; petiole 0.5-1.5 cm long, planoconvex in cross section, swollen at base, glabrous ; lamina 6-9 x 2.5-4 cm, elliptic to elliptic-obovate or sometimes spathulate , apex acuminate , base cuneate , margin distantly dentate and glands at the dentations, coriaceous , glabrous ; midrib raised above; secondary_nerves ca. 6 pairs; tertiary_nerves broadly reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary racemes ; flowers subsessile , white with purple tinge.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , globose , 1-1.5 cm across, green; seeds 1-2, rugose .

Ecology :

Understorey trees in evergreen forests between 500 and 1700 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the southern Western_Ghats- South Sahyadri.
