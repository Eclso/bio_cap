Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Large shrub or small trees , up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; stipules triangular, caducous ; petiole 0.9-1.3 cm long, canaliculate , glabrous ; lamina 8-13 × 4-7 cm, elliptic to obovate , apex shortly acuminate or acute , base cuneate to attenuate , margin serrate or serrulate , coriaceous , glabrous ; secondary_nerves 5-7 pairs; tertiary_nerves broadly reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers in clusters on short peduncle in the axils of fallen leaves .
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , 3-valved ; seeds 6, arillate .

Ecology :

Understorey tree in disturbed evergreen or semi-evergreen forests, up to 1000 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Central and Maharashtra Sahyadris.

Literatures :

Indian For. 103(6): 387. 1977; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 2: 97. 1996; Cook, Fl. Bombay 1: 232. 1902.
