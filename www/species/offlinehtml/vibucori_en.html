Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees , up to 8 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; stipule caducous ; petiole 2-3 cm long, canaliculate ; lamina 5-13 x 2.5-4 cm, ovate-lanceolate , apex long acuminate , base acute to slightly rounded , margin entire or slightly serrate , glands along the margin beneath the lower third of lamina ; midrib canaliculate ; secondary_nerves 4-6 pairs, the lower most ones subopposite , domatia hairy ; tertiary_nerves percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence compound umbel and corymbiform ; flowers greenish white, anther violet.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , ellipsoid , glabrous ; 1-seeded , compressed.

Ecology :

Along the margin of high altitude evergreen forests generally between 1600 and 1800 m.

Distribution :

India, Bhutan, Myanmar and Indonesia; in the Western_Ghats- common in Anaimalai and Palani Hills and also recorded from Coorg (Thalacauvery).
