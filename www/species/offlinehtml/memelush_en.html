Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees ca. 7 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 0.3-0.4 cm; lamina 3-8.7 x 0.8-4 cm, elliptic to narrow elliptic or obovate , apex obtuse or retuse , base acute to subattenuate , margin entire , green when dry; midrib canaliculate ; secondary_nerves and tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers pale blue, in small fascicles , axillary or lateral , shortly pedicellate .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , globose , 0.2-0.3 cm diameter, 1 seeded .

Ecology :

Generally understorey trees in dry evergreen forests, sometimes found in drier zones of wet evergreen forests along margins, between 300 and 1400 m.

Distribution :

Peninsular India; in the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.
