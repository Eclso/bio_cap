Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 12 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; stipules ovate to oblong , 0.7 cm long, caducous ; petiole 0.4-1 cm long, stout, planoconvex in cross section, pubescent when young; lamina 12-30 x 5-11 cm, oblong to elliptic-oblong , apex abruptly shortly acuminate , base rounded , margin entire or distantly serrate , glabrous , coriaceous ; midrib flat above; secondary_nerves 6-10 pairs, strong; tertiary_nerves strongly and broadly reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flower unisexual , dioecious , in axillary or lateral clusters, often on old wood , sessile .
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , subglobose , to 2.5 cm across, densely rufous hirsute , sessile ; seeds 2.

Ecology :

Understorey trees in low and medium elevation wet evergreen forests between 400 and 1400 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- South and Central (Coorg and Chikmagalur Regions) Sahyadris.

Literatures :

Kew Bull.23: 57. 1969; Gamble, Fl. Madras 2: 1302. 1993 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 415. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 2: 131. 1996.
