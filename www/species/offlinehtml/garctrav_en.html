Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 12 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 1-1.8 cm long, canaliculate , glabrous , slightly sheathing at base; lamina 5-11 x 1.3-2.5 cm, narrow oblong , apex obtuse , base acute , margin revolute , coriaceous , glabrous ; secondary_nerves nearly perpendicular to midrib , parallel ; tertiary_nerves finely reticulate , impressed above in older leaves .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers dioecious ; male flowers in terminal cymes ; female flowers one or two in upper axils.
Fruit and Seed :Berry oblong to subglobose with persistent style and broad stigma , usually 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Generally along the margin in medium elevation exposed stunted evergreen forests, between 1000 and 1400 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Agasthyamalai Hills.

Literatures :

Beddome, Fl. Sylv. 173. 1872; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 74. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 42. 2004.
