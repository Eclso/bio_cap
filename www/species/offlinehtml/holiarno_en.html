Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Large trees , up to 35 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral , clustered at twig ends; petiole 0.8-2.5 cm long with a pair of spurs (nearly glabrous and sometimes caducous ), planoconvex in cross section; lamina 10-25 x 2.5-7.6 cm, oblanceolate , apex obtuse to slightly acuminate , base cuneate to decurrent , margin entire , coriaceous , glabrous ; midrib raised above; secondary_nerves 10-20 pairs, tertiary_nerves reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary or terminal panicles , 12-24 cm long, golden yellow pubescent ; flowers polygamous , greenish white.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , obliquely ovoid , glabrous , rounded at apex , 3 cm long, wholly enclosed in obconic hypocarp , 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Canopy trees in wet evergreen to semi-evergreen and moist deciduous forests, up to 1200 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to Western_Ghats from Central and South Sahyadris.
