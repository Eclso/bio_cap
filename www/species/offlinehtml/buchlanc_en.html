Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees .
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral , crowded at the end of the branchlets ; petioles 1-1.5 cm long, glabrous , canaliculate ; leaf blade 5-11.5 x 2-3.5 cm, lanceolate or oblong-lanceolate , apex shortly acuminate , base cuneate , glabrous beneath, secondary_nerves 10-12 pairs, tertiary_nerves reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence terminal or axillary panicle , 5-7 cm long, finely rusty tomentose ; flowers bisexual , yellow; pedicels 0.5-1 cm long, pubescent , calyx ovate , pubescent ; petals glabrous , reflexed .
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , 1.5 cm across, compressed, glabrous , 1-seeded .

Ecology :

Rare, understorey tree in low elevation evergreen to semi-evergreen forests.

Distribution :

Western_Ghats (South Malabar and South Sahyadri) of India and Myanmar.

Literatures :
