Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 10 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole ca. 0.3 cm long, canaliculate , glabrous ; lamina 6-8.5 x 2-3 cm, lanceolate , apex caudate , base attenuate , margin entire , pellucid gland dotted, glabrous , pale beneath; midrib slightly canaliculate ; intramarginal nerve present; secondary_nerves ca. 12 pairs; tertiary_nerves admedially ramified .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary or extra axillary branched cymes .
Fruit and Seed :Berry ; seed one.

Ecology :

Trees along streams.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Belgaum Region of North Malanad (recorded from Kumbherwada and Castlerock).

Literatures :

Wight, Ill v. 2. p.16; Cook, Fl. Pres. Bombay. 1. 489. 1903.
