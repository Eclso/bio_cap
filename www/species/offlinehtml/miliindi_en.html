Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Shrubs or small trees , up to 7 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; petioles 0.1-0.2 cm long, pubescent ; lamina 5-7 x 2-2.7 cm, ovate , glabrous above, pubescent beneath when young, apex acute to obtuse , base rounded ; secondary_nerves ca. 12 pairs, faint; tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers solitary , axillary , white, fragrant; pedicels 0.7-1.5 cm long, glabrous .
Fruit and Seed :Clustered 2-seeded berries , subglobose , 0.7-1.0 cm across, red, glabrous ; stalk about 0.5 cm long.

Ecology :

Understorey tree in wet evergreen to dry evergreen forests, up to 1400 m.

Distribution :

India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.
