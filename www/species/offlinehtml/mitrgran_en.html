Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees , up to 12 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; petiole to 0.6-1.3 cm long, canaliculate ; ferruginous pubescent ; lamina 8-16 x 3.5-6.7 cm ovate to elliptic , apex acuminate with blunt tip, base acute to rounded , margin entire , coriaceous , dark green above, glabrous ; domatia hairy at axils of secondary_nerves ; secondary_nerves 6-8 pairs, prominent; tertiary and higher order nerves reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers solitary or 2-3 in leaf opposed cymes , pedicels ca.1 cm long, pubescent ; petals white to yellow.
Fruit and Seed :Clustered 1-2 seeded berries , globose , tomentose .

Ecology :

Understorey tree in low elevation evergreen forests, up to 600 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- rarely found in South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :
