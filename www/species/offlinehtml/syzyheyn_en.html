Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees up to 10 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole up to 1 cm long, planoconvex in cross section, glabrous ; lamina up to 10 x 2.5 cm, narrow oblong , oblong-lanceolate , apex obtuse or acute with blunt tip, base attenuate , margin entire , pellucid gland dotted, coriaceous , shining above, glabrous beneath; midrib slightly canaliculate above; intramarginal nerve present; secondary_nerves many, parallel , slender; tertiary_nerves admedially ramified .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence cymes , usually lateral , from the scars of fallen leaves , rarely axillary , flowers white, sessile or shortly pedicelled .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , oblong-ellipsoid , ca. 1 cm long, crowned by calyx lobes .

Ecology :

Along river banks, occasional.

Distribution :

India; in the Western_Ghats- throughout.
