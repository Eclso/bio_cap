Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees , up to 15 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; petiole up to 2 cm long, canaliculate , pubescent ; lamina 5-11 x 2.2-4.8 cm, elliptic to elliptic-lanceolate , apex acute to slightly acuminate with blunt tip, base acute to attenuate , margin entire , subcoriaceous , glabrous , white glaucous beneath; midrib slightly canaliculate ; secondary_nerves 10-13 pairs; tertiary_nerves closely reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers solitary , axillary , large, white or cream.
Fruit and Seed :Follicles , warty , arranged as spike , dehiscing dorsally ; seed 1, scarlet.

Ecology :

Common trees in high elevation to montane evergreen forests, between 1600 and 2400 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- Agasthyamalai, Megamalai, Anaimalai, Palani, Nilgiris and Bababudangiri Hills.
