Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , distichous , sometimes subopposite ; petiole 0.6-1.3 cm long, canaliculate ; lamina 5.5-11 x 3-4 cm, lanceolate , oblong-lanceolate , apex gradually tapering to acuminate with mucronate tip, base attenuate , margin entire , chartaceous , glabrous beneath; midrib canaliculate above; secondary_nerves 5-9 pairs, slender; tertiary_nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence terminal thyrsoid panicles , pubescent , up to 20 cm long; flowers white, fragrant.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , oblong , ca. 1 cm long, dark purple; seeds 1-3.

Ecology :

Along margin of evergreen to semi-evergreen forests up to 1800 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :

Saldanha & Nicolson, Fl. Hassan Dist. 513. 1976; Gamble, Fl. Madras 2: 797.1993 (re.ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 278. 2004.
