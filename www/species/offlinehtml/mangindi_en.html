Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees , up to 35 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral , clustered at twig ends; petiole 1.2-6.2 cm long, swollen at base, planoconvex in cross section; lamina 8-25 x 1.7-6 cm, narrow oblong-elliptic or lanceolate , apex gradually acuminate , base acute to attenuate , margin slightly undulate , subcoriaceous , glabrous ; midrib raised above; secondary_nerves many, nearly straight or gradually curved 28 to 30 pairs; tertiary_nerves reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence terminal panicle ; flowers polygamous , greenish white.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe , fleshy; single seeded , compressed.

Ecology :

Canopy trees in evergreen to semi-evergreen forests.

Distribution :

Wild species occur in Indomalaysian region and several varieties commonly cultivated throughout tropics.
