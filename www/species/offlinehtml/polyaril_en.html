Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Large shrubs to small trees up to 4 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; petiole 0.7-1.3 cm long, canaliculate in cross section, tomentose ; lamina 6.5-11 x 3-4.3 cm, elliptic , apex acuminate , base acute , margin entire , glabrous ; nervation impressed above; midrib canaliculate above; secondary_nerves ca. 8 pairs; tertiary_nerves broadly reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence racemes , to 15 cm long, pendulous ; peduncle to 3 cm long; pedicel 0.8 cm long; flowers zygomorphic , yellow; petals 3, lower one boat shaped.
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , obcordate , inflated; seeds 2, globose , compressed; carunlce orange-red.

Ecology :

Understorey trees in evergreen forests at medium elevation between 1200 and 1600 m.

Distribution :

Indomalaysia and south China- in the Western_Ghats- South Sahyadri.
