Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 12 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , spiral ; stipules 1.2 cm long, lanceolate , caducous ; petiole 7-26 cm long, terete , subglabrous , swollen at base; lamina 13-32 x 8-18 cm, distinctly peltate , broadly ovate to orbiculate , apex acuminate or sometimes acute , margin entire or minutely denticulate , subcoriaceous , glabrous above, pubescent and resinous yellow glands beneath; nerves up to 10, radiating from the centre; tertiary_nerves obliquely_percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flower unisexual , dioecious ; inflorescence axillary panicles with straight branches , bracts broadly ovate , dentate without apical glands ; male flowers in clusters per bract ; female flowers few per bract .
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , globose , covered with glandular scales , 1 or 2-valved cocci ; seeds globose , 1 per cocci .

Ecology :

Common heliophyllous in the openings of evergreen forests.

Distribution :

India and Sri Lanka; in the Western_Ghats- through out.
