Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 12 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; petiole up to 1.5 cm long, canaliculate , glabrous ; lamina 19 x 6.5 cm, oblong-lanceolate , apex acute to obtuse , base acute to attenuate , margin entire , coriaceous , glabrous , strongly reticulate on both surfaces; midrib canaliculate ; secondary_nerves ca. 8 pairs.
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers unisexual , dioecious ; male inflorescence 3-7 flowered axillary cymes ; female flowers solitary , axillary .
Fruit and Seed :Berry , globose , 4 cm across, reddish velvety, fruiting_calyx large, reflexed ; seeds 4-8, smooth, pulp viscid , turning black when exposed.

Ecology :

Along river banks.

Distribution :

Indomalaysia; all along the Western_Ghats .
