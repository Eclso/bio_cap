Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Small trees , up to 6 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , opposite , decussate ; petiole 0.5-0.9 cm long; lamina 2.5-8 × 1-5 cm narrow elliptic , elliptic , oblong-lanceolate , apex obtusely acuminate or acute , base acute , margin entire or distally serrulate near apex ; chartaceous , glabrous ; midrib thinly raised above; secondary_nerves 6-8 pairs, slender; tertiary and higher order nerves obscure .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers more than 7 in spreading axillary cymes , red.
Fruit and Seed :Capsule obcordate ; seeds 1-2 per cell.

Ecology :

Understorey tree in evergreen forests, generally between 700 and 1400 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to the Western_Ghats- South Sahyadri.

Literatures :

Roxburgh, Fl. Ind. 2: 410. 1824; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 203. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 96. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 2: 95. 1996.
