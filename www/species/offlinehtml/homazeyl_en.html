Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 25 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; stipules caducous ; petiole 0.5-1.3 cm long, glabrous ; planoconvex in cross section; lamina 7.5-13 x 3.6- 7.6 cm, elliptic , apex abruptly acuminate , base acute or rounded to subattenuate , margin crenate , chartaceous , glabrous ; midrib flat above; secondary_nerves 6-8 pairs, gradually curved; tertiary_nerves reticulo-percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence long, slender spikes with interrupted clusters of small flowers ; flowers generally greenish white, sometimes few clusters crimson red in the same spike .
Fruit and Seed :Capsule ; seeds small, many, oblong or angular .

Ecology :

Subcanopy trees in wet evergreen forests up to 900 m.

Distribution :

Western_Ghats and Sri Lanka; Western_Ghats- occasional in South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :

J. Linn. Soc. Bot. 4. 35.1860; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 522. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 32. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 1: 270. 1996.
