Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 8 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; petiole up to 0.5 cm long, planoconvex in cross section; lamina 4.5-8.5 x 1.5-3.5 cm, elliptic to elliptic-lanceolate , chartaceous , apex acute with blunt tip, base acute , glabrous ; midrib canaliculate ; secondary_nerves ca.11 pairs, tertiary_nerve closely reticulate .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers unisexual , dioecious ; male flower in axillary cymes ; female flowers solitary , axillary , with cream and hairy petals .
Fruit and Seed :Berry with fruiting_calyx ; 1-3 seeded .

Ecology :

In dry evergreen forests, up to 1800 m.

Distribution :

Peninsular India; in the Western_Ghats- leeward side of the South Sahyadri and eastern part of Nilgiris.

Literatures :

Sri Lanka J. Sci. Biol. Sci. 12 (2): 106. 1977; Gamble, Fl. Madras 2: 768. 1993 (re. ed).
