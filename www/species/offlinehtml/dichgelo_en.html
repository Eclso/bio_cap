Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Large shrubs or small trees , up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate , distichous ; stipule linear-lanceolate to 0.5 cm long; petiole 0.1-0.4 cm long, pubescent ; lamina 5-11.5 x 2-4 cm, elliptic , apex bluntly acuminate with mucronate tip, base acute , margin entire , chartaceous , sparsely pubescent on midrib and nerves beneath; midrib raised above; secondary_nerves 5-6 gradually curved; tertiary_nerves nearly horizontally reticulo-percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Inflorescence axillary , fascicled cyme ; flowers white.
Fruit and Seed :Drupe 1-2 lobed , pubescent , 2-seeded .

Ecology :

Understorey trees in evergreen to semi-evergreen forests up to 1400 m.

Distribution :

Indomalaysia; in the Western_Ghats- South and Central Sahyadris.

Literatures :

Engler and Prantl, Nat. Pflanzenf. 3 (4): 348. 1896; Gamble, Fl. Madras 1: 188. 1997 (re. ed); Sasidharan, Biodiversity documentation for Kerala- Flowering Plants, part 6: 92. 2004; Saldanha, Fl. Karnataka 1: 108. 1996.
