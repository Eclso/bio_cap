Botanical descriptions :

Habit :Trees up to 5 m tall.
Leaves :Leaves simple , alternate or subopposite , spiral ; stipules caducous ; petiole 0.5-1.5 cm long, glabrous , canaliculate ; lamina 6-19 x 2.5-8 cm, variable in shape from narrow elliptic to ovate , apex bluntly acute to acuminate , base asymmetric or rounded to acute , margin entire , chartaceous , glabrous ; midrib flat above; secondary_nerves 5-8 pairs, lowest pair opposite and acute in angle; tertiary_nerves broadly reticulo-percurrent .
Inflorescence / Flower :Flowers unisexual , monoecious ; male flowers in terminal slender racemes ; female flowers few at the base of the male racemes .
Fruit and Seed :Capsule , ellipsoid or subglobose , deeply 3 lobed ; seeds 3.

Ecology :

In wet evergreen to dry evergreen forests of Western_Ghats up to 900 m.

Distribution :

Endemic to Western_Ghats- mostly in Central Sahyadri.
